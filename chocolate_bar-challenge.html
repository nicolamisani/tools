🍫The Chocolate Bar Challenge

You are launching a new chocolate bar in a market of 1 million bars per year, against an established rival whose standard bar offers a willingness-to-pay of 100¢ at a unit cost of 60¢ and a shelf price of 60¢ (zero economic profit, 50 % starting share). You have four levers: pick recipe upgrades, decide on an efficiency cost cut that trades some benefit for lower cost, name your brand (a clever name gives a small extra benefit bump), and set your shelf price. A panel of consultants is on hand if you'd like a second opinion along the way.

Budgets: 5 recipe revisions, 5 cost cuts, 5 brand names, 5 price changes, 5 consultant briefings. Stop whenever you like by hitting Finalize. Your score is the profit at your final position.
